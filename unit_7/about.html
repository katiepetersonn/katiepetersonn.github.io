About

I (Katie Peterson) had a very clear goal in mind for 2016: to learn how to code. I am sharing my journey with you through Know the Code. The idea is to share inspiration, resources and tips with like-minded individuals who are trying to teach themselves to code.

I will be profiling individuals who work within the technology industry, gathering insights, busting stereotypes and sharing what I learn.

Launching in Sydney, Australia, in 2016 we will see how the adventure develops.

Don't hesitate to contact me if you would like to offer advice, would like advice or just want to have a chat.
